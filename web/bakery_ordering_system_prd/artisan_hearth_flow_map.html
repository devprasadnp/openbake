# Artisan Hearth: User Flow Map To ensure a seamless experience for both customers and administrators, I've organized the screens into two primary journeys. ## 1. Customer Journey: From Hearth to Home This flow covers the end-to-end experience for a customer discovering, customizing, and ordering their favorite treats. * **Discovery:** * **Home Page** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_10}}): The welcoming entry point. * **Menu / Listing** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_2}}): Browsing the full artisanal collection. * **Selection & Customization:** * **Product Detail** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_19}}): Deep dive into customization (size, flavor, special messages). * **My Wishlist** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_4}}): Saving favorites for later. * **The Transaction:** * **Cart Drawer** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_26}}): Reviewing the selection and applying promo codes. * **Checkout (Step 1)** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_15}}): Entering delivery and contact details. * **Order Confirmation** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_22}}): The celebratory final step. * **Post-Purchase & Account:** * **Order Tracking** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_17}}): Real-time updates on the baking progress. * **Order History** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_25}}): Reviewing past deliveries and quick reordering. * **My Profile** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_6}}) & **My Addresses** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_8}}): Managing personal details and delivery sanctuaries. --- ## 2. Admin Journey: Management & Fulfillment This flow represents the "Master Baker" experience, focusing on operational efficiency and business growth. * **The Command Center:** * **Admin Dashboard** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_20}}): A high-level view of KPIs and trends. * **Daily Operations:** * **Admin Orders** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_12}}): Managing incoming requests and fulfillment statuses. * **Inventory Management** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_24}}): Tracking flour levels and stock alerts. * **Curating the Menu:** * **Product Management** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_14}}): Adding and editing artisanal offerings. * **Categories Management** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_11}}): Organizing the menu for better discovery. * **Growth & Loyalty:** * **Admin Coupons** ({{DATA:SCREEN:SCREEN_9}}): Creating and managing promotional offers.
